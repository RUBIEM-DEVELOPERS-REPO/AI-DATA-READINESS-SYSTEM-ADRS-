1. Contact attribution
Main bug: persons and organizations are being assigned wrong emails, phones, addresses, and other contact details.

Add a strict contact-binding layer before final CDM commit.

Requirements:
- bind contacts only with strong local evidence
- use section boundaries, layout/block proximity, label cues, and ownership cues
- distinguish:
  - candidate personal contacts
  - organization contacts
  - reference contacts
  - document-level contacts
- do not assign one contact to multiple entities unless explicitly supported
- quarantine ambiguous bindings
- keep evidence and confidence per binding

Rules to enforce:
- nearest valid owner in same section/block wins
- reference section contacts must not attach to candidate
- employer/org contacts must not attach to person unless explicit
- header/footer repeated contacts must not attach to every entity
- email/name mismatch lowers confidence
- invalid ownership = reject or quarantine, not commit
